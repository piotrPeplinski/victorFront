Meet our team

Victor Gallery is a Gallery established by a son, a mother and closest friends. All of us are true art lovers, whose desire is to make art more inspiring in our life. The Gallery selects and sells the best works created by exceptional and recognisable artists. The Victor Team is consisted of the owner Wiktor Gaweł, graduated from marketing in Berlin, Hanna Piętka an art adviser who was raised and educated with artistic background, Piotr Pepliński a programmer with fresh vision and experience, Daria Halak a photographer whose picture was published in Italian Vogue edition and Anna Tyczyńska a professor at University of the Arts Poznan.
